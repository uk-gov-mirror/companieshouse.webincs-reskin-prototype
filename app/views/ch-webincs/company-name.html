What are the rules for company names?

Your company name:

  • can’t be the same as another registered company’s name
  • can’t be too similar to another registered name - but you may be able to get permission from the existing registered company
  • can't contain certain regulated words - unless you get permission
  • can't suggest a connection with government or local authorities
  • must not be offensive
  • can be up to 160 characters long
