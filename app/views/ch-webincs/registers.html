Where do you want to keep your company registers?

Company registers are records of:

  • directors’ details
  • secretaries
  • members
  • people with significant control

You can keep them at your company’s registered address or on the public record at Companies House.

Warning Information such as a director’s date of birth will be available to the public if you choose to keep your registers on the public record.
