Company name

The service will automatically check if the proposed name contains sensitive words and if it is considered to be the 'same as' any existing names on the Register. There is no guarantee that the name will be accepted.

The name cannot contain more than 160 characters (including spaces and name ending), and some symbols are not permitted. All new name letters are converted to capitals.
The name ending is automatically defaulted to LIMITED, however you can also choose LTD or LTD. if required. Welsh name endings are available for companies located in Wales.

Location of Registered Office

Please select:

IN01

  • By selecting this option you will be registering the information in English.
  • You will have an English memorandum.
  • You will be adopting English model articles for a private company limited by shares.
  • You will receive an English certificate of incorporation.

IN01c

  • By selecting this option you will be registering the information bilingually.
  • You will have an English memorandum and there will be a certified Welsh translation placed on the public record.
  • You will be adopting English model articles for a private company limited by shares.
  • You will receive both an English and Welsh certificate of incorporation.

Registered office address

Typically this c/o information consists of a person's name or the name of a company, partnership or firm whose property is being used as the service address for the company officer(s).
