How is this calculated?

The total value of the shares is calculated by multiplying the number of shares by the nominal value (face value) of each share.

New companies can set the face value of each share at the time of incorporation. Most start with small share values. For example, between £1 and £10 per share.

What rights can a share provide?

Most shares usually provide rights such as who can vote on company decisions and who is first to receive dividends. Custom share types may also give the holder additional benefits beyond company ownership.
